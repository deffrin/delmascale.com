Services

ESMA WORKSHOP

EMIRATES STANDAIZATION & METROLOGY (ESMA) WORKSHOP FOR CALIBRATION,REPAIR AND MAINTENANCE SERVICES

Balances should always be checked with reference weights you can rely on and trust.
We calibrate, adjust and document the results in a calibration certificate. The calibration services cover the basic reporting of conventional mass correction, uncertainty and traceability information in accordance with OIML-1994 requirements.

We undertake servicing and repair of all types of weighing scales and equipment.
We have a team of well-trained sales & service engineers who have through experience in servicing of all types of weighing scales and systems with minimum cost & absolutely or no downtime.

We are specialised in maintaining, servicing and calibrating all types & brands of weighing machines and systems of various capacities and applications.
We also provides installation and commissioning of all types of weigh bridges & nonstandard weighing equipment.
We undertake Annual Maintenance contract scheme for all types of weighing equipment including Batching Plants.
We have with us all necessary OEM spares to maintain these weighing scales mainly load cells and indicators.
